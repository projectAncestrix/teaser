FAQ ▼

• What is Project Ancestrix?
A community-driven ancestry project launching summer 2026. We collect participant submissions and analyze patterns of heritage, migration, and cultural identity.

• Who can join?
Anyone 18+ from any background. You do NOT need a specific ancestry breakdown.

• What do I need to submit?
Basic personal background, region/family origins, and (optional) genetic test results.

• Is this safe and private?
Yes — we only use your submission for internal study and no personal data is ever shared publicly.

• When will the project launch?
Full rollout begins summer 2026.

• Will I get updates?
Yes — participants will receive email updates as the project evolves.
